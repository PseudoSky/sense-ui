Sensor Visualization

These graphs show data from our distributed sensor modules. They've been running for only around 2 days, yet they already seem to show interesting patterns.

Ideally, we will use the regression analyses shown below to detect anomolies in the atmosphere that might inform the occupants of the building of strange or dangerous situations.

The Values We're currently tracking are Temperature, Humidity, Pressure, Butane (lpg), Methane (ch4), Carbon Monoxide (co), Light Intensity (luminance),
